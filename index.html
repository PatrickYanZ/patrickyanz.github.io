About Me

Zijiang Yan, BSc,2021, York University research assistant, under guidance of Prof. Hina Tabassum , I am currently working on modeling and optimizing of large-scale beyond 5G/6G multi-band wireless networks, where conventional sub-6GHz transmissions coexist with millimeter, TeraHertz with applications to aerial/vehicle networks. Work in Next Generation Wireless Networks Lab (NextG Lab)
