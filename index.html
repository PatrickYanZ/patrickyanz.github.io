Bio

I am a MASc student specializing in machine learning and GAI-driven wireless communication. I received my B.Sc. in Computer Science and Statistics from York University, where I pursue my master degree under the supervision of Prof. Hina Tabassum and Prof. Ping Wang.

Currently, my work explores resource-aware communication strategies, quantum-enhanced user association, and hybrid large language model (LLM)-assisted decision-making for joint driving and communication optimization.
