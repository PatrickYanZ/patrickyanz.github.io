Biography

M. Hossain Mohammadi (S’11, IEEE) received the Bachelor of Science degree in electrical engineering with the Valedictorian award from the American University in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, in 2013. He then received the M.Eng. (Thesis) degree in electrical engineering from McGill University, Montreal, QC, Canada, in 2015. He is currently working toward the Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering at the Computational Electromagnetics Laboratory, McGill University. His research interests include the design, optimization, and control of low-frequency electromagnetic devices. Since 2014, he has been an active volunteer in the IEEE Montreal Section.
