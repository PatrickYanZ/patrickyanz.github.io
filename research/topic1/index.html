Joint Optimization of V2I communication and Autonomous Driving on HighwayEnv

Jointly optimizing AV motion dynamics and wireless connectivity is critical to balancing the trade-offs between communication handovers and speed of AVs in order to achieve the following

  • Receive real-time traffic information from the wireless network

  • Access timely navigation data

  • Communicate effectively with surrounding vehicles

  • Predict dangerous situations and make informed decisions
